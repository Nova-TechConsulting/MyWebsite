About Parag Dessai

Founder & CEO, Nova-Tech Consulting

Parag Dessai is a thought leader in AI, automation, and low-code who helps enterprises turn strategy into shipped outcomes. He partners with executive teams to pinpoint where AI will move the needle, then leads delivery to design governed assistants, resilient automations, and modern apps that scale across the enterprise.

Parag’s experience includes engagements with top technology consulting and product organizations such as KPMG, IBM, Walmart, Unqork, and Microsoft. His cross-industry exposure—spanning financial services and technology—shapes a pragmatic approach to AI and Automation Adoption.

A renowned speaker at global conferences, Parag shares executive-level perspectives on AI Automation, AI governance, and the journey from experimentation to production—helping organizations place confident, ethical bets on AI.

If you’re exploring AI strategy, building an assistant, or standing up a COE, Nova-Tech can help you move from pilot to production—with measurable ROI.
